ساعد هيبة و عائلتها عل...Help Heba & Family Survive the Darkness of Gaza

My name is Abdul Haadi from Portugal, I’m writing to you on behalf of my sister Heba Mohammed, a brave mother of four soon to be five whose entire world has been shattered by war. For two long years, Heba and her children have lived through fear, hunger, and endless displacement. Their once beautiful home was reduced to dust. They’ve been forced to flee more than 30 times, sleeping in cold tents, praying each night for safety. Her husband was shot while trying to bring home food, leaving him unable to work or walk properly. Now, Heba is pregnant and exhausted, struggling to protect her children as winter approaches. She has no milk, no baby clothes, no blankets and often no food to quiet her children’s cries at night. Why your help means everything: Your donation isn’t just money it’s warmth for freezing hands, food for hungry children, and hope for a mother who refuses to give up. Every act of kindness can restore a piece of her broken world. If help doesn’t come: This family will face another cruel winter in the cold hungry, sick, and forgotten. Without your compassion, their suffering will deepen, and Heba will bring her baby into a world without shelter or safety. If we reach our goal: Heba’s family will finally have a safe place to live, medical care for her husband, warmth for her children, and the chance to start rebuilding their lives. Her children will return to school and believe in a future again. Please, if you’re reading this, take a moment to donate or share. Even the smallest gift can change everything for this family turning despair into hope and fear into peace. With heartfelt gratitude, Abdul Haadi
