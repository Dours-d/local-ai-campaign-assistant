I'm opening this campaign on behalf of Nurse Abdel-Rahim and his family who live in Gaza. here is their presentation : "Abdel-Rahim Louh is a dedicated nurse at Al-Aqsa Martyrs Hospital in Gaza. Every day, he works tirelessly to care for the wounded and sick, yet today, he and his family are the ones in desperate need of help. During the recent bombing, Abdel-Rahim’s home was completely destroyed, leaving him, his wife, and their four children with nowhere to go. With no other options, they are now living in a tent, facing harsh weather conditions and unsafe living circumstances. Despite his hardships, Abdel-Rahim continues to serve his community as a nurse, earning a modest salary that barely covers daily necessities. He even rides his bicycle to work every day because he cannot afford transportation. We are launching this campaign to raise $50,000 to: ✅ Rebuild Abdel-Rahim’s home so his family can live in safety again. ✅ Provide essential living expenses for his four children. ✅ Support his dedication to his lifesaving work as a nurse in Gaza. Imagine losing your home and having to raise four children in a tent. Every donation, no matter how small, will bring this family closer to rebuilding their lives. If you cannot donate, please share this campaign with your friends and family. Your kindness and generosity will change a family’s life. Thank you!"
