"My brother's name is Matar (Rain), but lately, the only rain we see is the tears in our mother's eyes." My brother, Matar, was always the light of our home. He is full of life, but a tragic accident took more than just his comfort—it took his eye. Since that day, his world has gone half-dark. In our current situation, the pain of his injury is compounded by a devastating reality: our family is struggling to find our next meal. The Mission: We are fighting two battles at once. First, we need to get Matar the specialized surgery he desperately needs to save what remains of his vision and restore his face. This surgery is not available locally; it requires travel and medical expenses that are far beyond our reach.
