Our Mission

Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF) provides critical support for the health, well-being, and education of Israel’s protectors.

 

We are the official organization authorized to collect charitable donations on behalf of the soldiers of the Israel Defense Forces across the United States of America.

About FIDF

Last year FIDF touched the lives of over 340,000 IDF soldiers, veterans, and their loved ones.

Our Impact

From 2023-2025, FIDF sent more than $325 million to Israel for the health, well-being, and education of Israel’s soldiers, reservists, veterans, and their families.
